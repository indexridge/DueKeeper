Privacy Policy

Due Keeper is designed to be local-first. The v1 app does not require an account, developer backend, tracking SDK, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or cloud sync.

Data you enter

Due-date records, reminder dates, categories, urgency labels, and task text are stored on your device. They are not sent to the developer by the app.

Sharing and export

If you choose to share or export a checklist, that action is handled by iOS and the destination you select. Review the destination app or service before sharing personal information.

Notifications

Due Keeper requests local notification permission only after you ask the app to schedule reminders. Notification permission is used for user-requested reminder-date and due-date alerts, not marketing.

Contact

Use the App Store support link configured for Due Keeper for privacy or support questions.
