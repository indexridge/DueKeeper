Due Keeper

Dates, reminders, and next tasks without another noisy to-do list.

Due Keeper helps you track renewals, return windows, warranties, passport checks, service dates, and admin deadlines with a clear countdown, an earlier reminder date, and one practical next task.

Countdowns that explain themselves

Every item shows days left, due today, or days overdue alongside the actual due date, so the countdown is useful instead of gimmicky.

Reminder date + due date

Choose an earlier reminder date and a due date. When permission is granted, Due Keeper schedules local notifications for both.

Fix stale tasks quickly

Reschedule changed dates and delete outdated task text without losing the underlying record.

Private by default

No account, developer backend, tracking SDK, advertising SDK, or cloud sync is required in this build.

Important safety note

Due Keeper organizes dates only. Notifications can be denied, silenced, delayed by Focus, or removed by iOS, so users remain responsible for checking important deadlines.

Due Keeper is a local-first date organization app. App Store pricing, availability, refunds, and distribution are handled by Apple.
