Due Keeper pricing and availability are configured in App Store Connect app pricing.

This build does not present in-app purchase, restore purchase, or recurring subscription controls. If the commercial model changes later, the app, App Store metadata, privacy disclosures, terms, and support pages must be updated together before submission.

Due Keeper is a local-first date organization app. App Store pricing, availability, refunds, and distribution are handled by Apple.
